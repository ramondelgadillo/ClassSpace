How Can We Help?

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the prompts to reset your password.

How can I edit or delete my post?

Click on your post and select 'delete' and confirm your action.

How can I report a comment or review that I think should be removed from your site?

Click on the review in question and click 'report' and the ClassSpace team will look into it.

What are Rewards Points and how do I redeem them?

Reward Points earn you benefits in the site. They are redeemed through your activity on the site.

How do I update my profile?

Go to your profile and click on 'customize' and then select customization options.

How do I post a review?

You can post a review only when logged in and can be done on the main page or in your personal profile.

Be considerate. We all want the best for our users to experience an easy way to obtain information from reviews through comment sections. This will promote a healthy user experience.

Be respectful. Users can disagree on their experiences, but there is no excuse for disrespect. An environment where people feel uncomfortable or threatened is not a one we will ignore.

Be inclusive. Be kind to others no matter their background.

Do not harass others. We disagree, understand why the opposing side thinks that way and move on. ClassSpace has zero tolerance towards harassment of any kind

If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us. You can reach us by email at student@student.csulb.edu, by phone at 000-000-0000, or by filling out the contact form on our website. We'll try to get back with you as soon as we can.
